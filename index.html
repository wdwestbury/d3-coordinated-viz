The State of Education in Wisconsin

Education Rates by County in Wisconsin

Long considered one of best states in the U.S. for public schools, Wisconsin ranks 4th out of all states in high school graduation rates with a state high school graduation rate of 88%. In addition to its high school graduation rates, Wisconsin is also known for high quality post secondary education institutions and is home to a top tier public research institution, The University of Wisconsin Madison. While the education rates in Wisconsin are high, they are not high everywhere and there are often clusters in both the highly educated and uneducated.
